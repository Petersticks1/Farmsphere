# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Title

FarmSphere – Agricultural Brand & Digital Platform

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** FarmSphere
**Product Type:** Agricultural Brand Website & Ordering Platform
**Purpose:**

FarmSphere is a modern agricultural brand focused on sustainable crop farming, livestock management, and reliable produce distribution. The website will act as a **digital storefront, brand communication platform, and customer engagement channel**, helping customers and partners understand FarmSphere’s operations, values, and product offerings.

The platform emphasizes **trust, quality, sustainability, and scalability** while keeping technical complexity low in the first phase.

---

## 2. Objectives & Success Metrics

### Objectives

* Position FarmSphere as a professional, technology-driven agricultural brand
* Showcase farm operations, products, and sustainability practices
* Enable retail and wholesale customers to place order inquiries
* Build credibility with partners and investors
* Provide a scalable base for future digital expansion

### Success Metrics

* Number of order inquiries submitted
* Partner and wholesale inquiry conversions
* Average time spent on Products and About pages
* Website traffic growth
* Repeat customer inquiries

---

## 3. Target Users

### Primary Users

* Retail customers seeking fresh farm produce
* Wholesale buyers (restaurants, hotels, retailers)

### Secondary Users

* Business partners and distributors
* Agro-investors and stakeholders
* Internal FarmSphere team (content updates only)

---

## 4. Scope of Product

### In Scope

* Marketing and informational website
* Product catalog (non-payment based)
* Order inquiry & subscription request forms
* Partnership inquiry system
* Blog / updates section (static)
* Mobile-first responsive design

### Out of Scope (Phase 1)

* Online payment gateway
* User authentication / dashboards
* Real-time inventory tracking
* IoT integrations
* Financial analytics portals

---

## 5. Core Website Features

### 5.1 Homepage

**Purpose:** Brand positioning and first impression

**Sections:**

* Hero section with headline: *Cultivating Quality. Sustaining the Future.*
* CTA buttons: Explore Our Produce | Partner With Us
* Overview of farm operations and sustainability focus
* Why Choose FarmSphere section
* Featured products categories
* Testimonials & partner logos

---

### 5.2 About Page

**Purpose:** Build trust and credibility

**Content:**

* Brand story and background
* Vision, Mission, and Values
* Commitment to sustainability and innovation

---

### 5.3 Products Page

**Purpose:** Showcase agricultural offerings

**Product Categories:**

* Fresh Vegetables
* Grains & Cereals
* Poultry Products
* Livestock Products
* Bulk Farm Produce

**Each Product Includes:**

* Name
* Category
* Description
* Availability (seasonal / bulk)
* CTA: Place an Order | Contact Sales

---

### 5.4 Partnership Page

**Purpose:** Attract strategic partners

**Target Partners:**

* Distributors
* Retail chains
* Food processors
* Restaurants & hotels
* Agro-investors

**Benefits Highlighted:**

* Structured supply agreements
* Quality assurance
* Consistent delivery planning

**CTA:** Become a Partner

---

### 5.5 Contact Page

**Purpose:** Communication and inquiries

**Contact Information:**

* Email
* Phone number
* Location (Nigeria)

**Forms:**

* General inquiry form
* Order inquiry form
* Partnership inquiry form

---

### 5.6 Blog / Updates Page (Optional)

**Purpose:** Thought leadership and updates

**Content Examples:**

* Sustainable farming practices
* Seasonal produce highlights
* Partner stories
* Company news

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

* Mobile-first responsive design
* Fast page load speed
* SEO-friendly structure
* Clean, professional UI
* Secure form handling

---

## 7. Technology Stack

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript (Vanilla)
* PHP (form handling & email notifications)
* Optional: Embedded maps

---

## 8. Future Enhancements (Not in Phase 1)

* Online ordering with payments
* Customer accounts
* Inventory dashboard
* Analytics and reporting
* Mobile application

---

## 9. Approval

This PRD defines the initial scope and requirements for the FarmSphere digital platform and serves as the foundation for design and development.
