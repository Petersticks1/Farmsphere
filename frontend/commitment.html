Commitment to Sustainability

FarmSphere takes its environmental responsibilities seriously. It is our policy to continually improve our farming systems and working standards
to meet our environmental, legal and social responsibilities. We have therefore participated in numerous schemes and initiatives.

Other Commitments

  • Plan the farms crop rotation and variety choice to minimise use of fertilisers, pesticides, herbicides and fungicides.

  • Umbilical slurry injection cuts our fertiliser down tenfold.

  • Maintain and enhance the structure of our soil through sustainable crop rotations and well managed operations.

  • Thoroughly research the source of our products, buying from nurseries and manufacturers who adhere to high quality and environmental standards.

  • Continually make sure our products come from sustainable sources, such as FSC certified woods and Fairtrade coffee in our cafes.

  • Recommend alternatives to chemical options where possible.

Recognition & Impact

FarmSphere Awards

FarmSphere is proud to be recognized for delivering quality produce, sustainable farming practices, and stronger support for local food systems in Nigeria. Every milestone reflects the hard work of our farmers, field teams, and partners.

Excellence in agriculture is not one harvest; it is a daily commitment to quality, responsibility, and people. At FarmSphere, we keep improving so every season creates better outcomes for farmers and communities.

FarmSphere Sustainability Recognition 2026

Recipient: FarmSphere Operations Team

Program: Climate-Smart Farming and Local Food Supply

Focus Area: Crop Quality, Poultry Standards, and Post-Harvest Efficiency

Highlights:

Expanded sustainable field operations with better soil care, efficient irrigation, and safer handling standards across farm activities.

Strengthened poultry and crop quality controls to improve consistency, traceability, and customer confidence.

Improved post-harvest and distribution workflows so produce reaches homes and businesses fresher, faster, and with less waste.
